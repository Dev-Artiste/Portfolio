About me

My name is Anthony Kiiru, born 1997 in Kenya. I am an inborn artist with a deep passion for design. I'm currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in Architecture at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT) in Kenya.

I often combine my talent for art with my passion for good (really — excellent) design. I discovered my passion for architecture at a young age and since then I haven't stopped pursuing my dream to become a leading architect of our time. I aim to redefine the built environment by bringing human emotion into their space.

Being naturally curious to know and understand, I love exploring different styles in my designs with the aim to address unique needs/ideas for different people. This has meant expanding my scope of study, and in return immensly improved my skill-set.
